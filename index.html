Hi, I'm Zach Danz

Front End Web Developer, Language Enthusiast, Lifelong Learner

Nice to meet you.

~ I’m a tech enthusiast who is characterized by my love of learning ~

After graduating with my Bachelor’s Degree in 2013, I moved to China to teach children in what would become a six year stint in the country.

My journey into the world of professional tech began in 2016, when I started working with a highly successful gaming company, MutantBox, as a Copywriter and Localizer. Later, I was taken on to work at one of the largest and most successful tech Start-Ups in all of Asia, Jiliguala, a digital English-language teaching APP, where I worked as an Educational Consultant and actor. At the same time, I completed my Master’s Degree in Intercultural Communication Studies.

Moving back to the United States, I felt like I had really only had a taste of what I could do in Tech. Wanting to learn more about the intricacies of Tech, I began my online TechDegree with Team Treehouse, where I began studying their professional courses on web development. I’ve now learned to build powerful websites with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and more!

I am fortunate that while living abroad, I was able to work a variety of different jobs. Having worked as a corporate trainer, a copywriter, a professional actor, a translator, an improv coach, a teacher, and more, I feel I can bring so much more to the table than just coding.

Besides programming, I love traveling (except during global pandemics), improvisational theatre, working out, reading, and can often found doing something new and wild on my wife’s YouTube channel.
